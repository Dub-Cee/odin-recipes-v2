Chicken Jook

Fresh bowl of Chicken Jook

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken Stock
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Peanuts
  • Green Onions
  • Dried Bean Curd Sheets

Steps

  1. Boil Chicken in stock pot to make chicken stock
  2. Remove chicken and set aside
  3. Add rice to chicken stock
  4. While rice is cooking, shred chicken
  5. Once rice has cooked, add chicken and bean curd sheets to porridge
  6. Let simmer for atleast an hour
  7. Top with green onions, soy sauce, and chili oil & enjoy
